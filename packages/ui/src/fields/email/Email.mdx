# Email field

An email field.

<Unstyled>

</Unstyled>

```jsx
<Email name="example1" placeholder="An example email field" />
```

## Validation

Email field can have restrictions (e.g. required). Error messages will be displayed upon violation of the configured restrictions.

<Unstyled>
  <Email
    name="email"
    label="Email"
    placeholder="john@doe.com"
    aid="Please enter your email"
    minLength={20}
    maxLength={500}
    width="300px"
    required
  />
</Unstyled>

```jsx
<Email
  name="email"
  label="Email"
  aid="Please enter your email"
  minLength={20}
  maxLength={500}
  required
/>
```

## Read only

Email field defined as read-only cannot be modified.

<Unstyled>
  <Email
    name="email"
    label="Email"
    placeholder="john@doe.com"
    value="John Doe"
    readOnly={true}
    width="300px"
  />
</Unstyled>

```jsx
<Email
  name="email"
  label="Email"
  placeholder="john@doe.com"
  value="John Doe"
  readOnly={true}
/>
```
